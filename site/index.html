kern.js

A super-simple bookmarklet for designers that finally puts you back in control of your website's typography.

Here's how it works: Add the bookmarklet to your browser and activate it while at your page. Then adjust your kerning, line-height, letter placement and more to your heart's content! When you're done, just copy the generated CSS and use it in your own stylesheet, right there alongside Lettering.js.

See? Suuuuuuper easy.
